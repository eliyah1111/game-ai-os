# Sprite Modes

## Asset Roles

- `character`: main gameplay avatar or mascot
- `enemy`: readable obstacle-like moving actor
- `helper`: assistant, pet, or companion
- `avatar`: profile or lobby character
- `animated_gameplay_asset`: object-like animated sprite

## Action Presets

- `idle`: subtle breathing, bob, shine, or anticipation
- `movement`: readable travel or bounce loop
- `jump`: anticipation, lift, peak, land
- `hit`: compact impact, recoil, recover
- `win`: celebration, sparkle, settle
- `lose`: squash, dim, recover or collapse
- `loop`: continuous idle-safe motion

## Sheet Presets

- `2x2`: four-frame compact loops and reactions
- `2x3`: six-frame richer casual animations
- `2x4`: eight-frame movement or polish loops
- `3x3`: nine-frame showcase idle or large mascot loop

Use a delivery atlas only after action sheets pass QC.
